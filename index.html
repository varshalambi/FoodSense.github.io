Aakanksha Lambi

Biomedical Engineering undergrad at Boston University, combining technical knowledge with business strategy to revolutionize food transparency.

"Today, health issues like diabetes, cancer, and obesity are on the rise, largely due to ultra-processed foods. Food labels are confusing and misleading—even experts struggle to decode them.

This lack of transparency keeps people from making informed choices. That's why we created FoodSense—an AI-powered app that deciphers complex ingredients, tracks real calories, and provides personalized insights for smarter food choices.

As health awareness grows, people need simple, reliable tools—AI makes that possible. Our vision is to revolutionize how people interact with food, empowering them to take control of their health."
